​​Our Lifelong Network

We have an unrivaled network with diverse placements spanning multiple asset classes, specialities, and teams. Not only is our presence in the financial services industry ever-expanding, but our members serve as emerging leaders in industries outside of finance. Our members work coast-to-coast and internationally as well. WOW members are members for life.Post-graduation, we serve as mentors, advisors, and friends in the industry.
Women on Wall Street welcomes "all who experience life through the lens of woman in body, spirit, identity - past, present, future, and fluid." Special thanks to the UC Berkeley Gender Equity Resource Center for this definition.
