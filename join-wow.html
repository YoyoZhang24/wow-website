YOUR CART

Start a WOW chapter at your university today!

Following the tremendous success of the inaugural Women on Wall Street chapter at the University of California, Berkeley, WOW is proud to expand its mission globally.

Our aim is to address gender inequality on Wall Street at a systemic level by creating inclusive spaces for ambitious young women interested in finance at universities everywhere.
​
We’re seeking passionate self-starters to help us grow. If you believe your campus would benefit from a Women on Wall Street chapter or a dedicated space for women in finance, please complete the application form below. We’ll be in touch soon!
Women on Wall Street welcomes "all who experience life through the lens of woman in body, spirit, identity - past, present, future, and fluid." Special thanks to the UC Berkeley Gender Equity Resource Center for this definition.
