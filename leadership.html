Meet Our Team

Abigail Copeland

Founder & CEO

Abigail is a M.A. Candidate at the Johns Hopkins School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS) where she is a pursuing a degree in international economics. She recently graduated from Berkeley with a degree in Chinese Language & Literature, where she completed her degree in 3 years. She has previously worked at the U.S. Department of Commerce, and as a sales & trading summer analyst at Standard Chartered Bank and Morgan Stanley. ​

Chloe Cullen

Co-Founder & COO

Chloe is a Berkeley Haas Business School alumnus with ambitions encompassing both entrepreneurship and finance. She was awarded UC Berkeley's highest honor as a Regents' and Chancellor's Scholar, 3-time Leadership Award Scholar, and the national Nashville Songwriters Scholarship from the BMI Foundation, 2021. Chloe works at Goldman Sachs as a Private Wealth Management Analyst in San Francisco. Personally, she a dedicated girls leadership advocate, and in 2020, she founded "The Girl Effect" Scholarship competition for entrepreneurial high school girls.

Robin Ying

Co-Founder & CFO

Robin is a recent graduate of Berkeley, where she majored in Business Administration. Robin is a 2023 Summer Intensive Girls Who Invest Scholar and was placed at J.P. Morgan in the Hedge Fund Solutions Group in New York during her sophomore year summer. She returned to J.P. Morgan during her junior year summer as a Global Asset Trading & Strategy Summer Analyst. She current works as an Investment Analyst at Cambridge Associates in San Francisco.​

Anna Ren

Co-Founder & Head of Strategy

​Anna is an undergraduate student at Berkeley, majoring in Mathematics and Rhetoric with a minor in Gender and Women's Studies. She is particularly interested in FemTech, focusing on developing solutions to improve women's health and wellness. Anna has actively engaged with FemTech founders and led initiatives to promote access to menstrual hygiene products. Outside of her professional pursuits, Anna enjoys writing, vlogging, and taking on leadership roles within the diverse community of International House, where she lives alongside people from 83 different countries.​
